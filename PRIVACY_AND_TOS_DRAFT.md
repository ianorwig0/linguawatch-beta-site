LinguaWatch is an independent educational browser extension and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Netflix, Max, YouTube, or their affiliates.

## Data Use Disclosure

When enabled, subtitle snippets may be processed by a third-party AI provider to generate translations, grammar guidance, and optional audio.

## Privacy Policy Clauses

- We process limited subtitle text from supported pages only when extension features are active.
- We use this data to generate lesson content and optional text-to-speech output.
- We do not sell personal information.
- We store extension settings and aggregate usage counters in browser extension storage.
- Users can disable the extension globally, per-site, and per-platform at any time.
