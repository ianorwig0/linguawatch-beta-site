# LinguaWatch Legal Risk Checklist

## Platform Terms Review

- Confirm extension behavior is permitted on supported platforms.
- Confirm subtitle extraction and overlays do not violate automation restrictions.
- Confirm no anti-circumvention risk based on DRM-protected playback.

## Copyright and Content

- Validate risk posture for processing short subtitle snippets.
- Keep snippets minimal and avoid transcript-level storage.
- Do not repurpose platform captions into reusable corpora.

## Branding and Disclosure

- Include "not affiliated or endorsed" wording in site and store listing.
- Avoid partner language unless written approval exists.

## Privacy and Compliance

- Disclose third-party AI processing for translation and TTS.
- Document lawful basis and user rights handling.
- Keep data retention short and purpose-limited.

## Security and Operations

- Ensure no hardcoded API keys in shipped bundles.
- Add platform kill switches and incident/takedown process.
- Keep an internal contact path for legal and policy requests.
